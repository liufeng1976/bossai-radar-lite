## What the Free License Covers

The BossAI Radar Lite Non-Commercial License 1.0 allows use for:

- internal technical evaluation;
- free non-commercial demonstrations;
- non-commercial modification and redistribution with the copyright and license preserved.

## What Requires Written Authorization

A separate written commercial license is required when BossAI Radar Lite is used for:

- paid SaaS, subscriptions or membership benefits;
- consulting, managed operations, intelligence reports or client delivery;
- paid courses, bootcamps or software bundles;
- internal business operations that directly support revenue;
- white label, OEM, resale or commercial redistribution;
- integration into a commercial product;
- paid hosting or managed services.

## What a Commercial License May Include

Depending on the agreed scope, a commercial license may include:

- defined legal commercial-use rights;
- the number of users, customers or deployments;
- private or managed deployment;
- implementation and training;
- advanced sources and industry templates;
- team permissions, notifications and CRM;
- white-label or redistribution rights;
- support terms and SLA.

A license does not automatically include third-party data rights, API fees, hosting, custom development or ongoing support unless the written agreement says so.
