## Positioning

**Lite** proves the method: public evidence, deterministic scoring, opportunity ranking and an actionable report on one machine.

**Commercial / Pro** turns the method into an operating system for teams, clients and revenue-generating workflows. The commercial edition is scoped through a written license based on users, deployments, customers, data sources, support and redistribution rights.
